Discrete Analog BJT-Based 555 Timer

Date: Spring 2026Role: Analog / PCB DesignerTools: KiCad, LTspicePrototype: Through-hole assemblyStatus: Complete

Overview

Recreated 555 timer behavior using discrete BJTs to demonstrate analog timing fundamentals and validate simulation-to-hardware workflow.

Objective

Replace a monolithic 555 IC with a transistor-level architecture and verify switching/timing behavior on real hardware.

Role

Owned simulation, schematic design, PCB layout, manufacturing handoff, component sourcing, assembly, and final completion.

Tools

LTspice for pre-layout simulation and KiCad for schematic capture and PCB implementation.

Design / Process

Partitioned the circuit into threshold/trigger comparators, flip-flop, output, reset, and discharge stages with labeled board regions for structured testing.
