BQ25570 Pendulum Energy Harvester PCB

Configurable energy-harvesting board for low-power multi-coil generation experiments.

Date: Spring 2026Role: PCB Design LeadTools: KiCad, LTspice, BQ25570Inputs: 6 channelsStatus: Complete

Overview

Designed a compact harvester board that converts intermittent low-level AC input from multi-coil pendulum generators into usable stored energy.

Objective

Create a repeatable platform for coil-routing experiments, low-power startup management, and future load validation.

Role

Led architecture definition, schematic capture, PCB layout, rectifier integration, connector planning, and iterative design refinement.

Tools

KiCad and LTspice with BQ25570-based harvesting and storage control stage implementation.

Design / Process

Organized six coil channels into a rectifier front-end feeding the BQ25570 stage, storage capacitor footprint, and output headers with short power paths and test-friendly access.
